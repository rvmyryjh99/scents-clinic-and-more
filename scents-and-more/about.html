About Scents Clinic & More

Luxury fragrances and accessories for the modern soul.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Mbabazi Peninah, Scents Clinic and More was born from a passion for quality fragrances and timeless elegance. What started as a small boutique has grown into a trusted destination for perfume lovers across Uganda.

We believe that scent is a powerful form of self‑expression. That’s why we offer a curated selection of perfumes, oil fragrances, and handpicked jewelry and cosmetics—all designed to help you own your aura.

Our CEO

Mbabazi Peninah leads the vision of Scents Clinic and More, ensuring every product meets the highest standards of quality and luxury.

Our Location

We are proudly based at Wandegeya Stanbic Bank, Kampala. Visit us to experience our collection in person.
